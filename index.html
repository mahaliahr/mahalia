My name is Mahalia

My name’s Mahalia (‘ma-hay-lee-yah’) I am a designer & creative, currently based in London. I have a keen interest in the multidisciplinary aspects of design. Particularly in the intersection between physical and digital design & the ways we can solve problems with emerging technology and new processes. Constantly driven by my curiosity, I always aim to keep an open-minded and user focused approach in my work – ensuring what I create is meaningful, functional and will have a positive impact.
